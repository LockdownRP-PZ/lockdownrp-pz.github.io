# Lockdown Ethos

**Lockdown RP** is first and foremost an **English-speaking, high-quality, semi-hardcore, serious roleplay server.** We cater to players who are willing to put in the extra effort to ensure their characters and actions are believable.

### What do we mean by this?

- **English-speaking**: We expect our users to communicate in proper English. Proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation are expected.
- **High-Quality**: We expect proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation. Roleplay should be of a high standard, meaning no single-word or joke responses, and roleplaying believable and three-dimensional characters while adhering to the rules of the server.
- **Semi-Hardcore / Serious Roleplay**: This is strictly a roleplay server, and roleplay takes precedence over gameplay. Character death happens, and we consider it a "positive", story-furthering element of roleplay. Your character and actions should be believable both during creation as well as in-game.

The worlds we craft together with players are deep, unforgiving, and feature permadeath. And though you get a single **Close Call Token** per season, we expect players to roll with and accept the punches it throws at them. Our staff team dedicates their time and skill to maintaining the community, custom mods, maps, and systems. Considering this, **we do not tolerate drama and toxicity**, and reserve the right to remove users at any time. 

This server is run by **volunteers**, and we expect users to behave maturely, in and out of game. **Privacy and discretion** are important to us, and we expect our community to trust our moderator and admin team. 

**We welcome suggestions**, but we hope you understand that you're playing on a privately-run server, and **we may not always agree.**

At the end of the day, at **Lockdown RP**, we want to create **good memories**, tell **great stories**, and have **a lot of fun** while doing so. We aim to be inclusive and foster a positive and chill community. **We hope you can join us in doing so too!**
